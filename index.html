PINK FLOYD

Pink Floyd is one of the most successful bands in history. The Dark Side of the Moon and The Wall, particularly, have been consistently hailed as some of the greatest albums ever created, not just financially. Band members changed over the years, their sound changed, and the band endured. With their incredible live shows and wildly imaginative albums, they have become known all over the world.Roger Waters, the album's chief songwriter, drew heavily on his own life to create Pink Floyd, the fictional protagonist in the album named after the band. Waters and Floyd both lost their fathers during World War II and had unhappy marriages.In addition, "Comfortably Numb" was inspired by Waters' experience taking a muscle relaxant in Philadelphia while battling hepatitis.

Final Cut

Pink Floyd's twelfth studio album, The Final Cut, was released in 1989. The album was recorded in several studios in the UK from July to December 1982. Roger Waters was the band's bassist and primary songwriter for the last Pink Floyd studio album.
